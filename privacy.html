Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026 · EchoTiles for iOS

EchoTiles does not collect any personal data. Your words, your trails, your game — they all stay on your device.

01

Overview

This privacy policy describes how data is handled within EchoTiles, a word puzzle game available on the Apple App Store. We believe your gameplay should remain private by default.

02

Data We Collect

None. As indicated in our App Store privacy label, EchoTiles does not collect data from users. There is no account registration, no email collection, and no tracking identifiers.

All gameplay data — including your echo trails, board memory, journey progress, daily streaks, and Zen Mode sessions — is stored locally on your device.

03

Third-Party Services

EchoTiles does not integrate third-party analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or external tracking tools. The app does not share any information with third parties.

04

On-Device Storage

Game progress and settings are saved on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data may be included in:

  • iCloud backups if you have iCloud Backup enabled in your device settings
  • Local device backups made through Finder or iTunes

You can manage or delete this data by uninstalling the app or adjusting your device backup settings.

05

Apple Services

When you download or update EchoTiles, Apple processes information related to that transaction according to Apple's own privacy policy. This includes App Store activity, Apple ID, and related services. That processing is independent of this policy.

06

Children's Privacy

EchoTiles is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. Parents and guardians can use Apple's Screen Time and parental controls to manage access.

07

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the effective date at the top of this page will change. For the most current data practices, always refer to the App Privacy section on the EchoTiles App Store listing.

08

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns about EchoTiles, please use the App Support link available on the app's App Store listing page.
