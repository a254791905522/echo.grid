A Board That Remembers

Unlike standard word games that reset after every round, EchoTiles introduces a living board experience. Trace words across a soft pastel board, connect neighboring letters, and build glowing paths that persist.

Some of your completed word trails remain visible and can return in future puzzles as gentle bonuses, reusable paths, and magical "echo" effects. The result is a word puzzle that feels more personal, more alive, and more rewarding over time.

Core Features

Living Echo Trails

Every valid word creates a glowing trail. Some trails are preserved and reappear in future puzzles.

Board Memory

The game remembers your previous play and turns it into visual and gameplay interactions on later boards.

Journey Mode

Daily Echo

Return each day for a fresh daily puzzle and build your streak over time.

Zen Mode

A calm, no-pressure experience focused on relaxing word discovery and satisfying visual feedback.

Signature Style

Gently tracks your puzzle habits and reveals a light player style profile based on how you play.
